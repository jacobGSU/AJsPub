AJ's Pub - Atlanta's Hot Spot for Food and Beverages

Who We Are

At AJ's Pub, we welcome friends, family, and fun times. Making memories is our passion, mixing drinks is our specialty. With a versatile menu, high tops, and pool tables, we are your one-stop shop to come enjoy the company good people. AJ's welcomes everyone above the age of 21.

Savoring the Moment

AJ's Pub hits the target with a variety of food items from appetizers to full meals. The food speaks for itself with savory flavors in our famous "Hungry Man Hamburger." All food is cooked to order, and locally grown. AJ's takes pride in using the most premium ingredients, so our customers don't have to worry about anything but who's going to sink the 8-ball.

Memories in the Making

AJ's Pub offers a karaoke stage, pool tables, and dart boards. Every second Tuesday of the month is Karaoke Night. The last Saturday of the month is Pool Paloza. Monday Madness Dart Tournament happens every week, with $5 cocktails and $2 shots.
